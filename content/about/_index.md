*The Great Cocky* is a short, illustrated allegory about how a flock can
talk itself into trouble — and how it might yet talk itself out again.
It's written for kids, and for the grown-ups who still read with them.

## The book

A parable in verse, told in the voice of an old kookaburra looking back
on the rise and fall of a charismatic cockatoo. It's about fear,
flattery, and the quiet power of people who keep their wits about them.

## Who made it

**Illustrations** — Paul Brandner.
